##  🦉 DMLab

DMLab works at the intersection of **data management**, **natural language processing**, and **human-centered AI**.

### 🚀 What We Do
We build end-to-end pipelines that transform **raw, messy data** into **structured, actionable knowledge**.

- 🌍 Web & social data mining and integration  
- 📊 Diagrammatic & structured data understanding  
- 📚 AI-assisted scientific literature analysis  

### 🔬 Research Areas
Our work supports data-driven discovery in:

- 🌱 Climate & environmental science  
- 🧠 Social computing & complex data ecosystems  
- ♿ Inclusive technologies  

### ❤️ Impact
We are particularly committed to **accessibility and inclusion**, including:

- 🗣️ Augmentative & Alternative Communication (AAC)  
- 🧩 Autism-related communication and accessibility
